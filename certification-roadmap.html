How It Works

1

Assess Your Skills

Complete a comprehensive assessment of your current cloud skills and experience across AWS, Azure, and GCP. Evaluate your knowledge in key domains like compute, storage, networking, and security to identify your strengths and areas for improvement.

2

Define Career Goals

Specify your career objectives, target roles, and professional preferences. Whether you're aiming to become a cloud architect, developer, or security specialist, we'll help tailor your certification journey to align with your aspirations.

3

Generate Roadmap

Receive a personalized, interactive certification roadmap with recommended certifications in logical progression. Our intelligent system considers prerequisites, difficulty levels, and your specific goals to create the optimal learning path.

4

Create Study Plan

Build a structured, time-based study plan with curated resources, realistic timelines, and achievable milestones. Access recommended courses, practice exams, documentation, and hands-on labs to maximize your success rate.

5

Track Progress

Monitor your learning progress, track completed certifications, and continuously update your skills assessment. Adjust your roadmap as you grow, ensuring your certification journey evolves with your career advancement.
